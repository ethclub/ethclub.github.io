About ETH CLUB

We are building a Decentralized community.

Decentralisation means distributing the power, functions, people or things away from a central authority or location. Historically the existence of anti authoritarian, self regulating and independent community has proven that these can exist providing mutual benefits to all. Now coming to the power of technology, Ethclub is built to empower a digital community with decentralised control means there is no central point of failure. The ethclub ecosystem has independent governance system with free form of communication. In this economy, one member benefits all other due to his action and consequently he also gets mutual benefits from others in the ecosystem.
